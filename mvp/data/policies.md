# Maison Beauté — Platform Policies

This document is part of the RAG knowledge base for the Beauty Advisor chatbot.

---

## Returns & Refunds

Pre-loved items are accepted for return within **14 days of delivery** if:
- The product does not match its described condition
- The item arrives damaged in a way not disclosed in the listing

Items must be returned in the same condition as received.  
Opened cosmetics are **non-returnable** unless there is a clear discrepancy with the listing description.

To initiate a return, contact us at returns@maisonbeaute.de with your order number and a brief description of the issue.

---

## Product Authenticity

All products sold on Maison Beauté are authenticated by our team before going live.  
We do not accept counterfeit items. Sellers who list inauthentic products are permanently banned.  
If you suspect a product may not be authentic, contact us immediately.

---

## Shipping & Delivery

- **EU shipping:** Standard 3–5 business days · Express 1–2 business days
- **International (outside EU):** Available for select destinations — rates shown at checkout
- Orders are dispatched within 1–2 business days of purchase
- Tracking details are sent to your registered email address automatically

---

## Allergen & Safety Disclaimer

Maison Beauté provides ingredient information as listed by sellers and on original product packaging.  
We are not responsible for individual allergic reactions.

**If you have a known allergy:**
- Check the full ingredient list on the product listing page before purchasing
- Perform a patch test before first use
- If you experience any adverse reaction after using a product, discontinue use immediately and contact us

**If you report an allergy or adverse reaction via our chatbot, you will be connected to a human team member immediately.**

---

## Seller Guidelines

Sellers must:
- List products honestly, including accurate condition ratings
- Provide a full ingredient list where available
- Disclose any damage, missing components, or significant wear
- Not list products that are expired or past their period-after-opening (PAO) date

---

## Privacy & Data

Maison Beauté processes customer data in accordance with GDPR.  
Your email address is never shared in chat. Order tracking details are sent directly to your registered email.  
For data requests, contact privacy@maisonbeaute.de.
