## 1. SYSTEM DESCRIPTION

The Maison Beauté AI Advisor is a four-module AI system deployed for a pre-loved luxury beauty marketplace in Berlin.

| Module | Function |
|---|---|
| Module 1 — Shop Manager Agent | Generates SEO product descriptions using Perplexity (ingredients) + Claude Haiku (copy). Human review required before publishing. |
| Module 2 — Beauty Advisor Chatbot | Conversational AI over product catalogue via Pinecone RAG. Hard-wired safety escalation for health/allergy mentions. |
| Module 3 — Customer Self-Service | Order tracking (zero PII in chat) + FAQ assistant over platform policies. |
| Module 4 — Newsletter Generator | Generates on-brand newsletters from trending topics. Multi-language. |

## 2. CLASSIFICATION REASONING (STEP BY STEP)

### Step 1 — Prohibited Practices (Article 5)?
❌ No. The system does not engage in:
- Social scoring of individuals
- Real-time biometric surveillance
- Subliminal manipulation
- Exploitation of vulnerabilities

→ **Not prohibited. Proceed to Step 2.**

### Step 2 — High Risk (Annex III)?

| Annex III Category | Applicable? | Reasoning |
|---|---|---|
| Critical infrastructure | ❌ No | Beauty marketplace, not infrastructure |
| Educational/vocational training | ❌ No | Not an educational system |
| Employment decisions | ❌ No | No hiring or HR decisions made |
| Access to essential services | ❌ No | Luxury cosmetics are not essential services |
| Law enforcement | ❌ No | |
| Migration/border control | ❌ No | |
| Justice/democratic processes | ❌ No | |
| Safety components of products | ⚠️ Partial | Allergy escalation module processes health-adjacent data |

**Assessment on health/safety (Module 2):** The chatbot detects allergy/health keywords and routes to a human — it does NOT diagnose, prescribe, or make autonomous health decisions. The AI acts as a triage flag only. This does not meet the Annex III threshold for high-risk.

→ **NOT High Risk. Proceed to Step 3.**

### Step 3 — Interaction with Natural Persons?
✅ Yes — Modules 2 and 3 interact with customers via chat interface.

### Step 4 — Generates Synthetic Content / Deepfakes?
❌ No — product descriptions and newsletters are operational business content, not targeted at individual persons in a deceptive way.

### Classification: **LIMITED RISK (Article 50)**

## 5. HUMAN OVERSIGHT MEASURES (Article 14 — Best Practice)

Although Article 14 formally applies to High Risk systems only, the following human oversight measures have been implemented as best practice:

| Measure | Implementation |
|---|---|
| Product description review | All descriptions return `status: pending_review` — never auto-published |
| Ingredient verification | `ingredients_verified: false` by default — human must confirm ingredients match physical packaging |
| Safety escalation | Allergy/health keywords trigger immediate email alert to founder — human responds within 2 minutes |
| Weekly LangSmith audit | All LLM interactions traceable and reviewable in LangSmith dashboard |
| Monthly keyword review | Safety keyword list reviewed and updated based on LangSmith data |
