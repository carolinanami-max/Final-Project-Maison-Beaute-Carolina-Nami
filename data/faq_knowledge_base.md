## What do New, Tested Out, and Pre-loved mean?

Maison Beauté classifies all products into three conditions:

**New**
These products are unused and in their original sealed condition — exactly as you would find them in a luxury beauty boutique. They have never been used.

**Tested Out**
These products have been minimally used — at least 90% of the original content remains. They are near-new and may show minimal signs of use.

**Pre-loved**
These products have been used but remain in very good condition. At least 50% of the original content remains.

All products — regardless of condition — are sold in their original packaging and professionally resealed after our quality and hygiene check.

## How does Maison Beauté guarantee product quality and authenticity?

Every product on Maison Beauté goes through a rigorous quality management process before it is listed for sale.

Our experts check:
- Authenticity: Is the product genuine and original?
- Condition: Does the product meet our quality criteria for its stated condition?
- Safety and shelf life: Is the product safe to use and well within its expiry date?
- Packaging integrity: Is the original packaging complete and undamaged?
- Hygiene: Is the product free from contamination?

Only products that pass all checks are listed on the platform.

## How is shelf life and expiry checked?

Shelf life is a core part of our quality assurance process. We use a multi-step approach:

1. PAO symbol (Period After Opening): We check the PAO symbol on the packaging, which indicates how long a product can be used after opening.
2. Expiry date: We verify the expiry date and ensure sufficient remaining shelf life.
3. Batch code analysis: We analyse the batch code printed on the packaging to verify the manufacturing date and formula version.
4. Sensory inspection: We check each product for changes in texture, colour, and scent that may indicate degradation.

Products that are close to expiry or show any signs of quality issues are not accepted for listing.

## Why is the batch number important?

The batch number (also called lot number or charge number) is printed on every cosmetic product by law under EU Cosmetics Regulation 1223/2009. It identifies the exact production batch of a product.

At Maison Beauté, the batch number is essential because:
- It allows us to verify the exact formula version (brands occasionally change formulas between batches)
- It enables precise shelf life calculation
- It supports ingredient traceability
- It is part of our commitment to transparency and safety

When a product is submitted for listing, the batch number is recorded and verified as part of our quality check.

## Why must products be in their original packaging?

The original packaging is a mandatory requirement for all products listed on Maison Beauté.

Reasons:
1. Ingredient information: The original packaging contains the full INCI ingredient list, required by law and important for customers with allergies.
2. Authenticity verification: The packaging is a key part of our authentication process.
3. Shelf life data: Manufacturing date, batch number, expiry date, and PAO symbol are printed on the original packaging.
4. Completeness: The original packaging ensures all components are present.
5. Luxury experience: Our customers expect to receive a luxury product in its original presentation.
6. Legal compliance: The original packaging is required to meet EU cosmetics labelling regulations.

Products without their original packaging cannot be accepted for listing.

## Which products cannot be sold on Maison Beauté?

For hygiene and safety reasons, the following products are excluded from our marketplace:

- Eye products: Any product that comes into direct contact with the eye (e.g. mascara, eyeliner, kajal pencils)
- Lip gloss and liquid lipsticks with applicators: Products with integrated applicators that contact the lips
- Nail polish: Excluded for safety reasons
- Open pot products (e.g. cream jars): Only accepted if factory sealed with intact protective film and no signs of use
- Any product past its expiry date

If you are unsure whether a product can be listed, contact our team at hello@maisonbeaute.de.

## Can I return a product if I am not satisfied?

Maison Beauté operates as a C2C (consumer-to-consumer) marketplace, meaning products are sold directly between private individuals. For this reason, returns are generally not possible.
